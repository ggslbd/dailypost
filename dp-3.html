Huaian, Jiangsu, China - April 5, 2024 [Note: no sound] Aerial video taken on April 5, 2024 shows flocks of birds perched on branches and foraging in the clear water at Baima Lake National Wetland Park in Huai 'an City, Jiangsu province, China. Trees, reeds and other plants also show the vitality of spring. In recent years, through the implementation of the Baima Lake National wetland back to the lake, clean water into the lake, dredging clean lake, ecological lake "four lakes project", the lake's net water surface expanded to 90 square kilometers, rich wetland resources and diversified ecological environment, to provide habitat for wildlife, become an important ecological protection area
